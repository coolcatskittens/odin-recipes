Pina Colada

Description

Try this tasty and creamy Pina Colada recipe!

Ingredients

  • 120ml pineapple juice
  • 60ml white rum
  • 60ml coconut cream
  • wedge of pineapple to garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Pulse all the ingredients along with a handful of ice in a blender until smooth. Pour into a tall glass and garnish as you like.
